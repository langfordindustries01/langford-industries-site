Inside the box

Pedalboard
Six swappable slots — compressor, octave, overdrive, fuzz, distortion, chorus, vibrato. Drag any pedal into any slot.
Time & tone
Stereo delay switchable pre- or post-amp, reverb, 10-band graphic EQ, noise gate and doubler.
